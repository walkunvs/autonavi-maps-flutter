## 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* Map rendering with `AutonaviWidget`.
* Camera control via `AutonaviController`.
* Support for Markers, Polylines, Polygons, and Circles.
* Custom `BitmapDescriptor` for marker icons.
* Map type selection (normal, satellite, night, navi).
* My location blue dot, compass, traffic layer, 3D buildings.
* Gesture controls (scroll, zoom, rotate, tilt).
* Coordinate conversion from WGS-84 to GCJ-02.
